Common Questions

How do the county slots work? ▼
Each county has a fixed number of subscriber slots based on how many skip-traceable leads are available — roughly one slot per 40 leads. Smaller counties may have just one slot (exclusive). Larger counties like Duval or Wayne can support dozens. When a county fills up it shows as "full" in the picker and is closed to new subscribers until a slot opens.
How often does the data refresh? ▼
Most counties refresh weekly — we re-pull public notice filings every 7 days. Florida counties (Duval, Clay, Nassau, St. Johns) refresh daily from the Jax Daily Record. Tennessee and Michigan counties update weekly as new filings are posted.
What does a skip trace actually give me? ▼
We run the property owner's name through multiple public data sources to find their current phone number and email address. Hit rate is around 65% — meaning roughly 25 of every 40 leads will come back with contact data.
Can I add or swap counties mid-month? ▼
Yes. You can add new counties at any time — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. To swap counties, cancel the one you're leaving (takes effect at period end) and subscribe to the new one. Contact us if you need help.
What counties are available right now? ▼
We currently cover {{ county_count }} counties across Michigan, California, Florida, and Tennessee. Michigan coverage is the deepest: Wayne, Macomb, Oakland, Genesee, Ingham, Kent, Jackson, Muskegon, Kalamazoo, Calhoun, Berrien, Washtenaw, Livingston, and more. Check the county picker above for real-time slot availability.
What exactly are the trial leads? ▼
Trial leads are real public-record filings — addresses, parcel IDs, amounts owed, sale dates — from properties where we don't have an owner name on file. Because there's no name, they can't be skip traced. We're transparent about that. Some investors use them to scout addresses or pull the owner name themselves through the county recorder. They're real data, just not the full package.
Is there a setup fee or contract? ▼
No setup fee, no contracts. The $1 trial gives you 7 days to see the data. County subscriptions are month-to-month and cancel any time through your account. Individual leads are also available at $5 each from the storefront without a subscription.
