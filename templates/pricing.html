Common Questions

How do the county slots work? ▼
Each county has a fixed number of subscriber slots based on how many skip-traceable leads are available — roughly one slot per 40 leads. Smaller counties may have just one slot (exclusive). Larger counties like Duval or Wayne can support dozens. When a county fills up it shows as "full" in the picker and is closed to new subscribers until a slot opens.
What's the difference between tiers? ▼
All tiers get access to the same county data and weekly refresh. The only difference is how many skip-traced leads are included each month — 8 (Starter), 25 (Professional), or 80 (Power). Additional leads are $10 each regardless of tier.
How often does the data refresh? ▼
Most counties refresh weekly — we re-pull public notice filings every 7 days. Florida counties (Duval, Clay, Nassau, St. Johns) refresh daily from the Jax Daily Record. Michigan counties update weekly as new filings are posted.
What does a skip-traced lead include? ▼
Phone number and email sourced from public records and people-search databases. Hit rate is roughly 65% — so plan on about 65 out of every 100 leads coming back with contact data. Your included allotment resets every billing cycle.
Can I add or swap counties mid-month? ▼
Yes. You can add new counties at any time — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. To swap counties, cancel the one you're leaving (takes effect at period end) and subscribe to the new one. Contact us if you need help.

Is there a setup fee or contract? ▼
No setup fee, no contracts. All plans are month-to-month and cancel any time through your account. Register free to browse the storefront — contact data stays blurred until you subscribe to that county.
