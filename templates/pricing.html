Common Questions

What does "no overlap" mean? ▼
If you subscribe to Wayne County, no other subscriber gets the same leads from Wayne. Each county is exclusive to one active subscriber at a time. If a county is taken, you'll see it greyed out in the storefront.
How often does the data refresh? ▼
Most counties refresh weekly — we re-pull public notice filings every 7 days. Florida counties (Duval, Clay, Nassau, St. Johns) refresh daily because the Jax Daily Record posts new filings every business day. Tennessee and Texas tax lists update monthly, Barry County tax auction quarterly.
What's included in the 7-day trial? ▼
You get access to 5–10 fully unlocked leads (real addresses, owner names, contact info where available) plus 5–10 skip trace lookups. One county, 7 days. No build fee, no setup cost — just $1. If you cancel before day 7 you won't be charged again.
Can I add or swap counties mid-month? ▼
Yes. You can add new counties at any time — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Swapping (dropping one, adding another) works the same way. Contact us and we'll sort it out.

Is there a build fee or setup cost? ▼
