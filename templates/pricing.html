Common Questions

What does "no overlap" mean? ▼
Smaller counties (under 200 available leads) are exclusive to one subscriber. Larger counties support a limited number of slots — for example, a county with 1,000 leads supports up to 5 subscribers, each getting a non-overlapping monthly allotment. When a county hits its slot limit you'll see it greyed out as "full."
How often does the data refresh? ▼
Most counties refresh weekly — we re-pull public notice filings every 7 days. Florida counties (Duval, Clay, Nassau, St. Johns) refresh daily because the Jax Daily Record posts new filings every business day. Tennessee and Texas tax lists update monthly, Barry County tax auction quarterly.
What's included in the 7-day trial? ▼
You get access to 5–10 fully unlocked leads from one county for 7 days. Skiptraces are available at $5 each during the trial. No build fee, no setup cost — just $1. If you cancel before day 7 you won't be charged again.
Can I add or swap counties mid-month? ▼
Yes. You can add new counties at any time — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Swapping (dropping one, adding another) works the same way. Contact us and we'll sort it out.

Michigan coverage is the deepest: Wayne, Macomb, Oakland, Genesee, Ingham, Kent, Jackson, Muskegon, Kalamazoo, Calhoun, Berrien, Washtenaw, Livingston, Ottawa, Saginaw, and Barry. Check the storefront to see current lead counts per county.
Is there a build fee or setup cost? ▼
No build fee at this time. The $1 trial gets you in the door, and monthly subscriptions start at $199 for your first county. That's it.
